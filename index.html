Our History

The Flax Bourton Hub is a modern and well-equipped one storey building and associated small playing field in the North Somerset village of Flax Bourton

It is served by a dedicated service road, good footpaths and a cycle track which is part of the Sustrans national network. There is also a small children’s play area nearby

Historically, the facility was built and provided by Persimmons Builders over 20 years ago, as part of a quid pro quo arrangement with the Village resulting in the build of a medium sized housing estate which partially wraps around the field.

The Hub provides an important and contrasting community facility which balances and greatly extends opportunities for residents to pursue a range of interests, activities and sports in a pleasant environment.

The village has a strong record of promoting social events with many different activities taking place throughout the year. These are supported by residents of all ages. Prior to the Lock-down periods many of these took place at the Hub.

The responsibility of managing the Hub has recently passed from a Community Interest Company back to the Parish Council. The Council are working with a group of local volunteers to restore and develop the facilities. A new Charitable Incorporated Organisation was established on September 14th 2021 with trustees drawn from local residents. The Registered Charity Number is 1195804

The Hub seeks to be inclusive of our diverse community. We welcome all races, ethnicities, religions, genders, sexual orientations, disabilities, languages and ages.

In collaboration with the Parish Council, The Hub has recently commissioned a community consultation survey seeking views on village life generally, how the Hub and its activities might specifically be best used to improve living in Flax Bourton for all its residents. Please note some key points for all hirers

Party Time

Why not have your party at the hub? We are licensed for music and dancing until 11pm Monday to Sunday

Noise

The hub is close to residents and every effort must be made to limit noise when entering or leaving the hub. Amplified music must be turned off and doors/windows closed by 11pm

Toilets

Male and female toilet facilities are available only for hirers of the main hall. Users of our sports field do not have access routinely to the toilet facilities unless by prior arrangement. Please email bookings@flaxbourtonhub.org to enquire

Payments

You will be required to pay for your hire before your event. We will be offering Paypal for your convenience and will send you an invoice via email when the facility is active. You may be required to pay a deposit for some types of event

Fixings

Please do not fix or hang anything on to the walls of the building.

Cleaning up

Please make sure you wash up and tidy the kitchen surfaces and remove your trash after your event. This will help the deep covid clean that needs to take place after your event.

Security

You must make sure that all windows and doors are securely locked after your event
